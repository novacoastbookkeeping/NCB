Our Services

Everything You Need to Keep Your Finances Afloat

We offer comprehensive bookkeeping services tailored to Canadian businesses, ensuring you stay compliant and profitable.

Daily/Monthly Bookkeeping

Accurate recording of all financial transactions, keeping your general ledger up-to-date and organized.

Bank Reconciliation

Matching your business books with your bank statements to catch discrepancies and prevent fraud.

HST/GST Filing

Timely preparation and filing of your sales tax returns to keep you compliant with the CRA and provincial guidelines.

Payroll Processing

Ensuring your employees are paid accurately and on time, including source deductions and T4 preparation.

Accounts Payable & Receivable

Managing the money coming in and going out, sending invoices, and paying bills to maintain healthy cash flow.

Catch-Up Bookkeeping

Behind on your books? We provide clean-up services to get your past months or years organized and tax-ready.
